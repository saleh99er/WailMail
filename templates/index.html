Wail Mail

Tutorial

Hi, Welcome to Wail Mail, the web app that plays an audio file if an email that matches a rule arrive. The rules reserve '(', ')', 'and', 'or', and '^'. Every other word separated by a space will be a term checked in your emails. Specifically from, subject, body, and multipart portions of your email. If that word occurence is seen (case insensitive but must be an exact word match), then that word will be substituted with True in your rule, False otherwise. What's located on the right of the `arrow` is assumed to be your audio file located within static/audio of the machine running this program. Lastly, using python logical operators you can set pseudo-python logical expressions such as the following.

hello and world -> screaming_sheep.mp3

Now every time an email arrives with word occurences of 'hello' and 'world', the screaming_sheep.mp3 will be played. Note previously arrived emails that match this will not trigger this event even if left unread.
